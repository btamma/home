Prototypes, Testbeds & Tools

Developed CogAP (intelligent Wi-Fi AP device which automatically selects best channel to operate on) and UCSD-CogNet system (testbed of two dozen Wi-Fi channel monitoring nodes for spatio-temporal characterization of Wi-Fi traffic) at UC San Diego, USA campus. Testbed of SDN for Wi-Fi load balancing, testbeds of LTE-Wi-Fi interworking (LWIP & LWA), LTE-Wi-Fi co-existence, and Cloud-RAN using OAI platform, a prototype of Telepresence Robo for Healthcare (TRH) and Min-o-Mee and SafeNav Android Apps at IIT Hyderabad campus. Open source contributions on LWIP on OAI platform, LTE Energy Module in NS-3, and gRPC-SBA based 5G Core.
